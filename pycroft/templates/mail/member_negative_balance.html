Dear {{ user.name }},

We hereby inform you that your balance is {{ -user.account.balance }}€.
Your negative balance may be caused by the following reasons:

(1) Your payment has not reached us, yet.
If you used the correct personalized payment description, the
transaction should be assigned automatically to your account during the
next few days.

(2) You have not payed your membership contribution, yet.
In that case, please transfer the due amount immediately.
Make sure to use the correct payment description.
If your balance is still negative after the 14th of this month, your connection
will be blocked.

(3) The payment description of your transaction was not the correct
personalized one so your payment could not be assigned automatically to
your account.
In that case we ask you to send us a proof of payment via email [1].
It has to contain the target IBAN, amount, payment description and day
of payment.

If you have moved out and forgot to send us your membership termination,
please note that you can cancel your membership any time (but not
retroactively) without giving reasons
* on our website [2]
* via email [1]
* in person during our office hours [3].
When terminating your membership, the contribution for the month is due
on the day of termination [4].
Please also be aware that you have to pay your debt in any case.

Best Regards,
Your AG DSN
